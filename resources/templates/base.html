ABOUT FASHION BABA


Fashion is about expressing yourself & not just looking good. Fashion is about what you believe in & what you stand for. Sustainable fashion is needed now in a way where you can style your outfits, as well as support the environment. Fashion is about co-existence. The idea is to adopt a sustainable lifestyle in everything & letting go of fast fashion, which is the 2nd most pollution causing industry in the world.

Mission

Our mission is to provide a sustainable fashion brand, spread awareness on sustainable fashion & to support designers by providing them an accessible platform to showcase & grow in the industry.

Vision

Our vision is to be the go-to brand for sustainable fashion & cater to the needs of every consumer who wants to adopt a sustainable lifestyle. We aim to build a strong community of designers, enthusiasts & influencers promoting & preaching sustainable fashion.

WHY FASHION BABA

PROBLEM

Pollution caused by Fashion Industry-2nd highest pollution causing industry in the world

SOLUTION

Sustainable clothing: clothes made of sustainable/natural fabrics like jute, hemp, cotton, linen, wool etc.

PROBLEM

Lack of Awareness about sustainable clothing lifestyle & how can we contribute at an individual level?

SOLUTION

Blogs, videos, podcasts on educating/ spreading awareness on things related to sustainable fashion, styling your existing outfits in different ways, etc. available on a dedicated mobile app.

PROBLEM

Expensive Fashion-Design Education.


SOLUTION

Design to Earn (D2E) platform for budding fashion designer: Design your outfits, get feedback from experts in the industry & get your outfit produced under FB | Public Fashion House

PROBLEM

Lack of sustainable custom clothing options.


SOLUTION

One on one outfit consultation with Fashion Baba team delivering your Dream outfit to your doorstep!

PROBLEM

No worldwide community Fashion house


SOLUTION

Community driven Public Fashion house to bring inclusiveness of different fashion styles
